Support

Help with creating, editing, exporting, and protecting your travel journals.

Photo access

WanderJournal uses Apple's system photo picker. Tap the + button, optionally add photos, and choose only the photos you want to use. The app does not browse your full library without your selection.

Private AI

Photo understanding and journal writing run entirely on your iPhone. If the Private AI download is not installed, open Settings and download it over Wi-Fi. After setup, creating and polishing journals does not call a cloud AI model, use an API account, or consume paid AI tokens.

Missing place details

WanderJournal uses only GPS metadata embedded in selected photos. If no photo GPS is available, the journal remains usable but omits place and map claims. An internet connection is required to look up nearby place context.

iCloud backup and restore

When you are signed in to iCloud, saved journals can sync through your private CloudKit database. Reinstall the app and use the same Apple Account to restore synchronized journals. Downloaded AI files and original voice audio are not backed up.

Export or delete data

Open Settings to export all journals or permanently delete all journals. When iCloud sync is active, journal deletions synchronize to your other devices. Individual entries can also be shared or deleted from their detail screen. Sharing creates an image locally and sends that file through the iOS share sheet; WanderJournal does not upload the journal or create a public link.

Contact

Email info@easynet.world and include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem. Do not send private photos unless you intentionally choose to.
