Support

Help with creating, editing, exporting, and protecting your travel journals.

Photo access

WanderJournal uses Apple's system photo picker. Tap Create a Journal and choose the photos you want to use. The app does not browse your full library without your selection.

Apple Intelligence

Richer on-device writing is available only on compatible devices where Apple Intelligence is enabled. WanderJournal automatically uses its private offline writer on other devices.

Missing place details

Check your internet connection for nearby-place lookup, or use the manual place search. Photos without location metadata can still be used in a journal.

Export or delete data

Open Settings to export all journals or delete all local data. Individual entries can also be shared or deleted from their detail screen.

Contact

Email info@easynet.world and include your iPhone model, iOS version, and a short description of the problem. Do not send private photos unless you intentionally choose to.
